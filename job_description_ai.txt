Job Title: AI / Machine Learning Engineer (Graduate / Early Career)
Job Summary

We are seeking a motivated and technically strong AI/ML Engineer to design, develop, and deploy scalable machine learning solutions. The role involves working on real-world AI systems across computer vision, NLP, federated learning, and data engineering. The ideal candidate has hands-on experience with modern ML frameworks, privacy-preserving learning techniques, and end-to-end pipeline development, gained through internships and applied industry projects. 

Key Responsibilities

Design, train, evaluate, and optimize machine learning and deep learning models for real-world applications.

Develop computer vision solutions using CNNs and object detection models such as YOLOv8.

Implement privacy-preserving and secure machine learning techniques, including federated learning and encrypted aggregation (BFV, CKKS).

Build and maintain scalable data pipelines for model training, validation, and deployment.

Apply feature engineering, hyperparameter tuning, and model optimization to improve accuracy and inference performance.

Develop and integrate NLP solutions, including chatbots and prompt-based systems, using modern frameworks.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams in agile environments to translate business requirements into AI-driven solutions.

Document models, pipelines, and experiments clearly for maintainability and knowledge transfer.

Required Skills & Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.

Strong programming skills in Python with hands-on experience in ML/DL workflows.

Experience with machine learning and deep learning libraries such as TensorFlow, Keras, and scikit-learn.

Practical knowledge of CNNs, DNNs, and object detection frameworks (YOLOv8).

Understanding of federated learning, secure aggregation, and privacy-aware ML systems.

Proficiency in SQL and experience with data engineering concepts and pipeline optimization.

Familiarity with NLP, prompt engineering, and tools such as LangChain or IBM Watsonx.

Experience working in cloud or hybrid environments (AWS preferred).

Strong analytical, problem-solving, and debugging skills.

Preferred Qualifications

Internship or project experience in AI/ML engineering, data science, or applied research roles.

Exposure to federated learning with encryption schemes such as BFV and CKKS.

Experience building production-oriented ML pipelines or real-time inference systems.

Knowledge of agile development practices and collaborative engineering workflows.

What You’ll Work On

Computer vision systems for surveillance, detection, and classification.

Secure and privacy-preserving AI systems for distributed learning environments.

NLP-driven automation tools and intelligent assistants.

Data engineering pipelines supporting large-scale ML workloads.

Location

Bengaluru, India (Hybrid / On-site depending on project needs)
